Jyoti is delighted to cater food and drinks to your function! Whether it be a graduation party, Sweet 16, or engagement reception, among other types of events, Jyoti can offer a great experience pairing your event with a taste of fine Indian cuisine. It's a match made in heaven!

Jyoti can substitute using other ingredients to ensure that all food and drink items give you the same exquisite taste of Indian cuisine you are looking for. No matter if it's just appetizers or a full assortment of Indian food and drink items, Jyoti offers the ability to make sure your event functions smoothly with our array of products ensuring you get an amazing taste of India.

Banquet Hall Information

Jyoti welcomes you the opportunity to celebrate your momentous occasion in our massive banquet hall. No matter if it's an event of just 40 or as many as 150 people, Jyoti can make your night a memorable one cherished for life. Jyoti welcomes outside DJs and even food from another restaurant to be placed in the banquet hall. Additionally, you are more than welcome to bring your own drinks, but our bar features an assortment of alcohol from standard to sophisticated liquor products. An open bar can be accommodated as well with just soda products.

Note that because of high demand, slots fill up easily. Jyoti recommends you book at least a month in advance to host your event in the banquet hall. Nevertheless, we ensure your event will be spectacular given that silverware and other fine china, as well as chairs and tables, among other standard items held in a banquet hall, are already provided for your use. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What type of menus do you offer?

▶
Jyoti bases its catering and banquet hall menu on that of the restaurant, as well as through food delivery partners GrubHub, Uber Eats, & Doordash. Jyoti also has an exclusive dessert menu for events. If you contact us before your booking, we can revise and accommodate the menu to suit your party's dietary preferences and restrictions, such as offering vegetarian foods only.

How much food can you accommodate for larger events to be catered?

▶
For work meetings and other events, there is no limit on the amount of food ordered. Though we are busy with the number of guests who order catering from the restaurant, Jyoti can send a tray of just fresh appetizers to a full assortment of Indian food. We provide all drinks except for alcohol products.

How much time in advance do you need from us to notify you of making a catering order?

▶
Since we cater around 5 events per day on average and serve a large number of customers inside the restaurant every day, Jyoti requires at least a two-week notice to ensure your event goes smoothly. It is still acceptable to call during a time period shorter than that, though we have the right to deny your event given our availability. Do know that Jyoti is extremely flexible and will do everything to cater your event!

How far away can you arrange catering orders?

▶
3 team members from Jyoti will typically drive to your event and ensure the set-up process and food/drink displays are set up accordingly. Jyoti can cater to your party within a 25-mile driving distance. This means all of Wayne is covered, and includes areas like Parsippany, Randolph, Fairfield, and New York City, among others.

Does the banquet hall allow for events to be hosted if there are as less as 20 people?

▶
Because our space is very spacious in size, we try to accommodate parties with 40 people as the minimum. Typically, Jyoti does not allow for exceptions to be made. However, you are more than welcome to mention this to the caterer helping you, and we will try our best to accommodate this request.

Do your staff members handle the full set-up for events at the banquet hall?

▶
At a minimum, Jyoti sets up the displays for food and drinks, as well as the restaurant ambiance. This includes the arrangement of seating and tables. You are more than welcome to ask us to change the displays in a way you feel better serves your event. For DJs and other external vendors such as the food and drink from other restaurants, they are responsible for setting up their materials accordingly.

Does the banquet hall operate on holidays such as Memorial Day, July 4, and Christmas?

▶
The banquet hall hours can be found here, and they are consistent throughout the calendar year. So long as the date of the holiday matches the banquet hall hours specified on the days of the week, then there should be no problem hosting your event.

Who can I contact for more information?

▶
You can call the restaurant number specified on this webpage. It is consistent for the general restaurant, as well as for the catering and banquet hall offered at Jyoti. Do specify on the call that you are asking for more details about catering or hosting an event in the banquet hall to speak with the relevant team member.
