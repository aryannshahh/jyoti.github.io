Our Story

Jyoti, derived from the Hindi meaning of "divine light," is the cornerstone of fine Indian dining in the New York tri-state area. What started out as a local pasttime making dishes for owner Aman Banerjee quickly turned into opening his own restaurant after the immense praise received from his customers. By 1994, the restaurant officially opened to the public in the heart of Wayne, New Jersey, allowing people around the New York City area to have easy access to explore the delicious flavors Jyoti offers.

Jyoti is an Indian restaurant known for its premiere assortment of foods from samosas and raita, but is much more than just that. The restaurant offers an approach to select from non-contemporary dishes such as lamb curry, shrimp bhindi, and even sham swera, among others. The bar is filled with an assortment of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages as well to celebrate the end of the work week, or to enjoy the big game with your friends on our televisions. In addition to the luxury cuisine, creating appetizing options for an array of consumers with different tastes and preferences, Jyoti is also known for its banquet hall. Events from Sweet 16's to receptions and other festivities can be held in our massive banquet hall. It's a match made in heaven with fine Indian dining and your event! Jyoti also offers catering for customers to enjoy Indian dishes at home; no matter which option you choose, our staff ensures that you will be satisfied and have your needs taken care of.

Our commitment to you is:

  • Delivering only the highest quality assortment of food and drinks to our customers.
  • Using produce that is locally sourced and does not pose a risk to consume.
  • Preserve the image of classic Indian food whilst also catering to the newer generation of food lovers using aesthetic ambiance and decor.
  • Ensuring the highest possible customer satisfaction.
  • Creating a culinary experience that will have you coming back for more.

At Jyoti, know that this isn't just any Indian restaurant. It's a family-owned gem that has been appreciated for many generations. And we'd love for you to be a part of this tantalizing experience. We are proud to welcome you into our family; thank you!
